News

BELIV 2016
The BELIV workshop series is a bi-annual event focusing on the challenges of evaluation in visualization. Oct 2016, the sixth version of BELIV will take place. BELIV 2016 will be co-located IEEE VIS 2016 in Baltimore, Maryland, USA, and as always be open to all conference attendes.
Stay tuned! More details will follow soon!

Announcement
UBC just announced a new framework that provides, among other things, libraries for psychophysical experiments on various kinds of visualizations. The idea is to lower the entry barriers for anyone who wants to get involved in those kinds of studies. An overview and contact information can be found in this summary slide.

BELIV 2014 announced
The BELIV workshop series is a biennial event focusing on the challenges of evaluation in visualization. BELIV 2014 will be the fifth of the BELIV workshop series and will be held Nov 10, 2014, as a one-day workshop at IEEE VIS 2014 in Paris. All registered attendees of VisWeek will be able to attend the workshop.
